Title :  Unveiling the Spectrum of Rest: Exploring Seven Transformative Dimensions
In her enlightening TED Talk (refer : https://ideas.ted.com/the-7-types-of-rest-that-every-person-needs/) , Dr. Saundra Dalton-Smith expounds on the significance of rest beyond conventional notions of sleep. Drawing from her medical expertise and personal experiences, she introduces seven transformative dimensions of rest that encompass various aspects of our lives. By recognizing and embracing these seven dimensions of rest, individuals can cultivate a holistic approach to self-care and well-being. Dr. Dalton-Smith's insights provide a framework for understanding the transformative potential of rest in all its forms, ultimately leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
